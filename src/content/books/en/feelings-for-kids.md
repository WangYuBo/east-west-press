*"I didn't mean to lose my temper… I just couldn't control it."*
*"Why can't I stop watching these videos?"*
*"I knew the answer, but I couldn't raise my hand."*

If your child has ever said these things, **this book is for them.**

## What Is *Feelings for Kids*?

*Feelings for Kids* is a **comic-style psychology guide** for children aged 8–12. It takes the confusing, messy emotions of everyday life and explains them through **real-life school and family stories** — no boring textbooks, no big fancy words.

Using **fun illustrations and relatable characters**, it reveals the **brain science and psychology** behind why we feel, act, and react the way we do. Each short chapter is like a little key that unlocks a mystery: *"Oh! That's why I get so angry so fast."* *"Oh! That's why deep breaths actually help when I'm nervous."*

## What's Inside?

Six big themes with 24 bite-sized lessons:

- **Explore Your Inner World** — Changing moods, personality differences, growth mindset, confidence, and self-acceptance
- **Getting Along at School** — Making friends, resolving fights, classroom vibe, and talking with teachers
- **Psychology in Groups** — Conformity, peer pressure, emotional contagion, group dynamics, and speaking up
- **Emotional Energy at Home** — Parent-child communication, family rituals, safety, and handling parents' arguments
- **How Kids Feel Online** — Short-video addiction, gaming, likes, online identities, and staying safe from rumors
- **Mental Strength on the Path of Growth** — Exam anxiety, social comparison, competition, courage, and building a positive mindset

## Why This Book Works

- **Visual & Engaging** — Every page is illustrated like a graphic novel. Kids *want* to read it.
- **Real Psychology, Made Simple** — Concepts like *mirror neurons*, *amygdala hijack*, *emotional contagion*, the *Pygmalion effect*, and *growth mindset* are explained through stories and metaphors.
- **Practical Tools** — Each lesson ends with easy, actionable tips (e.g., the 3-second pause, deep-breathing exercises, self-talk scripts) that kids can use immediately.
- **Relatable Scenarios** — From classroom debates to family dinners, from group chats to sports competitions — every story feels like it could happen to *your* child.
- **Covers Both Offline & Online Life** — Addresses screen time, social media pressure, and online safety with clarity and kindness.
