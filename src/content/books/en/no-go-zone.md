Originally published in Chinese, *The No-Go Zone* is a practical, no-nonsense safety guide that empowers children to recognize hidden dangers in everyday situations — and act decisively to get out fast.

There are too many risks in the world to avoid every pitfall. Children's safety codes need to be updated for the realities they face today: from navigating public spaces and school environments to understanding boundaries with strangers and acquaintances alike.

Written in clear, age-appropriate language and grounded in real-world scenarios, this book gives kids the awareness and confidence they need — without fear-mongering. It's a must-read for parents, educators, and anyone who cares about keeping children safe in an increasingly complex world.

Translated from the original Chinese by Eva.
